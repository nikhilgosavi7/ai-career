About this Application

This AI Career Adviser is designed to help you explore potential career paths based on your academic level, interests, and skills. By following a simple, step-by-step process, you can receive personalized recommendations and detailed information on how to pursue your dream career in India.

The recommendations are based on popular and in-demand career fields after the 10th and 12th standards, including various specializations in Engineering, Medical, and Management. Each career path includes an estimated salary range, educational requirements, and a clear career progression pathway to guide you.
